Ohrid
Ohrid is a charming city, also known as the pearl of Macedonia, and beautiful Ohrid pearls are worn even by Queen Elisabeth II. Discover the most beautiful attractions of Ohrid, a city located on the shore of the picturesque Lake Ohrid at the foot of the Galicica Mountain Range. The uniqueness of this region is proven by the fact that it has been listed in the UNESCO World Heritage List since 1979. Its treasures of architecture and diverse nature make it an ideal summer destination.
